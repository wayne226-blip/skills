# Your AI Brain — Getting Started

## What is a claude.ai Project?

A Project is a space inside Claude where your business instructions and documents are always loaded. Every time you start a chat inside your Project, Claude already knows your brand, your tone of voice, and how you like things written. No need to explain yourself every time.

## Setting Up Your Project

1. Go to **claude.ai** and sign in
2. In the left sidebar, click **Projects**
3. Click **Create Project** (or the + button)
4. Name your project: **{{BUSINESS_NAME}} Brain**
5. You'll see two sections: **Project Instructions** and **Project Knowledge**

## Adding Your Instructions

1. Click into the **Project Instructions** box (sometimes called "Custom Instructions" or "System Prompt")
2. Open the file called **project-instructions.md** that we sent you
3. Select all the text (Ctrl+A or Cmd+A) and copy it (Ctrl+C or Cmd+C)
4. Paste it into the Project Instructions box (Ctrl+V or Cmd+V)
5. Click **Save**

That's it — Claude now knows your business, your brand voice, and your rules.

## Starting a Chat

1. Make sure you're inside your **{{BUSINESS_NAME}} Brain** project (check the project name at the top)
2. Click **Start a new chat**
3. Type what you need — Claude will automatically use your brand voice and business context

**Important:** Always start chats from within your Project. If you start a chat from the main Claude screen (outside the Project), Claude won't have your instructions.

## Tips for Best Results

- **Be specific** — "Write a follow-up email to Sarah about her kitchen quote" works better than "Write an email"
- **Give context** — The more detail you provide, the better the output. Paste in notes, bullet points, or rough drafts
- **Start new chats for new topics** — Each chat is a separate conversation. Don't try to do everything in one chat
- **Paste in examples** — If you have a piece of writing you love, paste it in and ask Claude to write in that style
- **Iterate** — If the first draft isn't quite right, tell Claude what to change: "Make it shorter", "More formal", "Add a call to action"

## If Something Doesn't Sound Right

If Claude's output doesn't match your brand voice:

1. **Check you're in the right Project** — Look at the project name at the top of the screen
2. **Give more direction** — "Make it sound more [casual/formal/warm/direct]"
3. **Share an example** — Paste in something you've written that you like, and say "Write more like this"
4. **Let us know** — We can update your Project Instructions to fix recurring issues
