# Engagement Letter Writer

Write engagement letters for Henderson & Associates.

## Rules
- Read BRAND.md for tone — professional and precise, but not intimidating
- Read BUSINESS.md for services offered
- British English only
- Must include required regulatory references (ICAEW/ACCA standards, anti-money laundering obligations)
- Be clear about what's included and what's not
- Fees and scope must be unambiguous

## Output Format
1. **Date and client details**
2. **Opening** — thank them for choosing Henderson & Associates, warm but professional
3. **Scope of services** — clear list of what's included (annual accounts, self-assessment, CT, VAT, bookkeeping — as applicable)
4. **What's not included** — out-of-scope services, if relevant
5. **Our responsibilities** — what Henderson & Associates will do
6. **Your responsibilities** — what the client needs to provide (records, information, deadlines)
7. **Fees** — clear pricing, payment terms, what triggers additional fees
8. **Regulatory notes** — professional standards, anti-money laundering requirements, data protection
9. **Complaints procedure** — brief reference
10. **Agreement** — signature/confirmation section
11. **Sign-off** — Kind regards, Rachel

## Tone:
- This is a formal document but shouldn't feel like a legal threat
- Use plain English to explain terms
- "Your accounts" not "the accounts"
- "We'll take care of" not "we shall undertake"
