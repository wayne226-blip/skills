# Aloha Protein Bars — Ad Creative Summary

## What Was Done
1. Researched the Aloha brand (plant-based organic protein bars) using existing knowledge of the brand, its products, positioning, and visual identity.
2. Selected 5 ad formats best suited for a protein bar brand running paid social and digital ads.
3. Generated 5 detailed image prompts formatted for Nano Banana Studio bulk import.

## Format Rationale

| # | Format | Ratio | Why This Format |
|---|--------|-------|-----------------|
| 1 | Flat Lay Ingredient Spread | 1:1 | Instagram feed staple for food brands. Showcases clean ingredients — Aloha's biggest differentiator. |
| 2 | Lifestyle Action Shot | 9:16 | Stories/Reels/TikTok vertical format. Shows the product in-use during an active lifestyle moment. |
| 3 | Minimal Product Hero | 4:5 | Facebook/Instagram feed optimal ratio. Premium brand feel with negative space for text overlay. |
| 4 | Texture/Break Shot | 16:9 | YouTube pre-roll, display ads, website hero banners. The "money shot" that sells taste and quality. |
| 5 | Morning Routine Scene | 4:5 | Feed ad showing the product in daily life context. Relatable aspiration for the target demo. |

## Prompt Design Choices
- **Model:** flux-1.1-pro across all prompts for consistent high-quality photorealistic output
- **Style:** All prompts lean into natural light, warm earth tones, and clean compositions to match Aloha's organic/wellness brand identity
- **No logos or text in prompts:** Text overlays are better added in post-production for accuracy
- **Ingredient visibility:** Multiple prompts highlight real ingredients (almonds, cacao, coconut, pumpkin seeds) since "clean label" is Aloha's core selling point
