# BRAND DNA DOCUMENT

## BRAND OVERVIEW

- **Name:** ALOHA
- **Tagline:** "The Better Plant-Based Protein Brand" / "Good For You and the Planet"
- **Design Agency:** Not publicly confirmed; brand aesthetic aligns with Hawaiian-inspired modern wellness design
- **Voice Adjectives:** Warm, Playful, Purposeful, Approachable, Confident
- **Positioning:** Premium organic plant-based protein bars that taste indulgent while being clean-label. Employee-owned Certified B Corp focused on sustainability and Hawaiian heritage.
- **Competitive Differentiation:** USDA Organic + Non-GMO Project Verified (competitors rarely have both), B Corp certified, employee-owned, Hawaiian cultural roots with Special Edition bars sourced from Hawaiian farms, partnership with Kupu youth conservation nonprofit. 14g protein with 5g sugar or less — positioned as the "tastes amazing AND is actually clean" bar.

## VISUAL SYSTEM

- **Primary Font:** Clean modern sans-serif (headline weight: bold/heavy uppercase for brand name; body: regular weight sans-serif)
- **Secondary Font:** Occasional serif or italic serif for editorial pull quotes and sub-headlines
- **Primary Color:** Deep teal/green (#1B6B5A approximate — used on packaging backgrounds, nav elements)
- **Secondary Color:** Warm cream/off-white (#F5F0E8 approximate — backgrounds, cards)
- **Accent Colors:** Rich chocolate brown (#3C2415), coral/warm red for CTAs, gold/amber for highlights, and flavor-specific colors on packaging (purple for PB Cup, green for Chocolate Mint, etc.)
- **Background Colors:** Warm cream, white, light sage/green tints
- **CTA Color and Style:** Solid rounded-rectangle buttons, typically dark green or black on cream backgrounds, white text

## PHOTOGRAPHY DIRECTION

- **Lighting:** Bright, natural, warm daylight — studio shots are clean with soft shadows; lifestyle shots are sun-drenched and warm
- **Color Grading:** Warm and slightly golden; earthy tones, natural greens, rich chocolates
- **Composition:** Clean and airy with generous white space; product shots centered with ingredient props scattered around; lifestyle shots feature hands, outdoor settings, active but relaxed moments
- **Subject Matter:** Protein bars (wrapped and unwrapped showing texture), ingredient close-ups (chocolate, peanut butter, berries), hands holding bars, outdoor/nature settings with Hawaiian influence
- **Props and Surfaces:** Natural wood, marble, clean white surfaces; scattered ingredients (chocolate chips, nuts, coconut flakes, fruit); organic textures
- **Mood:** Joyful, sunlit, healthy-indulgent — like a treat you feel good about

## PRODUCT DETAILS

- **Physical Description:** Individually wrapped protein bars in matte finish wrappers. Each flavor has a distinct color wrapper with the ALOHA logo prominently displayed. Bars are coated (chocolate, white chocolate) with visible texture (cookie pieces, nuts, etc.)
- **Label-Logo Placement:** ALOHA logo centered on wrapper front; flavor name below; nutritional callouts (14g protein, organic, etc.) on front panel
- **Distinctive Features:** Organic certification badges visible on packaging; rich texture visible when unwrapped; coated exterior
- **Packaging System:** Individual bars, 12-count boxes. Flavors include Peanut Butter Cup, Chocolate Chip Cookie Dough, Chocolate Cherry (limited edition), Cookies and Creme (limited edition), Chocolate Mint, Coconut Chocolate Almond, Vanilla Almond Crunch, Chocolate Fudge Brownie. Also Mini Bars, Protein Drinks, and Protein Powders.
- **Key Stats:** 14g protein, ≤5g sugar, 10g fiber per bar. ~$2.92/bar at 12-count ($34.99). 4.7-4.9 star ratings. 7,600+ reviews on sampler pack. Available in 20,000+ stores nationwide.

## AD CREATIVE STYLE

- **Typical formats:** Clean product hero shots, comparison tables (vs "The Other Guys"), certification badge strips, flavor-forward food photography, UGC-style lifestyle content
- **Text overlay style:** Bold uppercase sans-serif headlines, clean and minimal, high contrast (white on green or dark on cream)
- **Photo vs illustration:** Predominantly photography; icons used for certifications and benefits
- **UGC usage:** Social handle @alohamoment featured prominently; community-driven content on Instagram/TikTok
- **Offer presentation:** "Save up to 25% with Autoship," "Free shipping on $40+," 15% off email signup coupon

## COMPETITORS

1. **GoMacro** — Also organic and plant-based, but less polished brand identity, less emphasis on taste indulgence
2. **No Cow** — Plant-based, higher protein (21g), but uses stevia (ALOHA is stevia-free), more clinical/gym-oriented positioning
3. **RXBAR** — Clean label transparency ("No B.S."), but not organic, not vegan, more utilitarian branding
