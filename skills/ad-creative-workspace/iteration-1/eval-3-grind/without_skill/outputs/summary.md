# Summary — Grind Coffee Ad Prompts

## What was done

1. **Brand research** — Compiled a profile of Grind (grfrnd.com) covering visual identity, product range, target audience, and brand voice. Web tools were unavailable, so research was based on existing knowledge of the brand. Saved to `brand-research.md`.

2. **Prompt generation** — Created 8 image generation prompts tailored to Grind's aesthetic and saved them as `prompts.json` in Nano Banana Studio bulk-import format.

## Prompt breakdown

| # | Concept | Ratio | Use case |
|---|---------|-------|----------|
| 1 | Flat lay of compostable pods on pink marble | 1:1 | Feed ad / product focus |
| 2 | London apartment lifestyle with pink cup | 4:5 | Instagram/Facebook story ad |
| 3 | Overhead espresso crema close-up | 1:1 | Feed ad / engagement |
| 4 | Subscription box unboxing moment | 4:5 | Story ad / DTC conversion |
| 5 | Farm-to-cup split composition | 16:9 | Facebook banner / landscape ad |
| 6 | Graphic pop-art style overhead cup | 1:1 | Bold feed ad / brand awareness |
| 7 | Cyclist with pink travel cup in London | 4:5 | Lifestyle story ad |
| 8 | Dramatic pour shot with pink Moka pot | 4:5 | Premium brand story ad |

## Design decisions

- **Signature pink** threaded through every prompt to maintain brand consistency
- **Mix of ratios:** 1:1 for feed ads, 4:5 for Stories/Reels placement, 16:9 for banner/landscape
- **Model:** All set to `flux1.1-ultra` for highest quality output
- **Themes covered:** Product detail, lifestyle, sustainability story, unboxing/DTC, urban London identity, bold graphic style
- Prompts avoid text/logos (AI image generators handle those poorly) — text overlay should be added in post-production

## Limitation

Web search and fetch were unavailable during this session. The brand research is based on training knowledge and may not reflect the very latest product launches or campaign direction. Worth cross-checking against the current grfrnd.com before final use.
