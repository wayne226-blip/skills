PROMPT 1 — Dramatic Gym Training Shot

A young athletic woman in fitted seamless leggings and a cropped sports bra performing a deep barbell back squat in a dark, industrial gym. Warm overhead spotlights cut through haze, casting dramatic golden beams across her body. Sweat glistens on her shoulders and arms. The background is moody and dark — exposed brick walls, iron plate-loaded racks, rubber flooring. Her expression is focused and powerful. Camera angle is low, shooting slightly upward to emphasize strength. The clothing fits perfectly, highlighting the seamless construction and body-contouring design. Cinematic fitness photography with high contrast, dark shadows, and warm highlights. Shot on 85mm lens, shallow depth of field.
