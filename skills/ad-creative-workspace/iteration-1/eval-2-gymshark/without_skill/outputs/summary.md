# Summary — Gymshark Ad Prompts (Without Skill)

## What Was Done
Created 3 Higsfield-ready ad image prompts for Gymshark based on brand research.

## Brand Research
Web search tools were unavailable, so research was conducted from existing knowledge of Gymshark's brand identity, visual style, product lines, and advertising approach. Key brand traits identified: dark/dramatic gym photography, seamless activewear as hero products, Gen Z/young millennial audience, high-energy motivational tone, DTC-first model.

## Prompts Created

1. **Dramatic Gym Training Shot** — Woman performing barbell squat in moody, dark gym with dramatic lighting. Emphasizes the seamless leggings fit and athletic intensity. Cinematic style.

2. **Lifestyle Street-to-Gym Transition** — Young man in joggers and hoodie walking through an early-morning urban setting, heading to the gym. Bridges Gymshark's streetwear-meets-performance positioning. Cool editorial tones.

3. **Product Detail Hero Shot** — Macro close-up of seamless legging fabric mid-movement, showing knit texture and compression construction. Berry-mauve colorway. Studio product photography.

## Output Files
- `brand-research.md` — Gymshark brand overview, visual identity, audience, positioning
- `prompts.txt` — 3 plain-text prompts formatted for Higsfield
