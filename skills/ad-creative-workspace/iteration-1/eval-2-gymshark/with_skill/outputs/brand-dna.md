BRAND DNA DOCUMENT
==================

BRAND OVERVIEW
Name: Gymshark
Tagline: "Be a Visionary" / "United We Sweat"
Design Agency: In-house creative team (previously worked with external agencies for campaigns; brand refresh led internally)
Voice Adjectives: Bold, Empowering, Community-Driven, Aspirational, Youthful
Positioning: Premium-accessible fitness apparel brand built on influencer culture and community. Bridges the gap between elite athletic performance wear and streetwear aesthetics. DTC-first, social media native.
Competitive Differentiation: Unlike Nike or Adidas, Gymshark was born on social media and YouTube — the brand IS the community. More affordable than Lululemon, more fashion-forward than Under Armour. Known for flattering fits (especially leggings/shorts), seamless construction, and drops that sell out.

VISUAL SYSTEM
Primary Font: Gymshark Sans (custom proprietary sans-serif, bold condensed, all-caps for headlines — similar to Knockout/Bebas Neue weight)
Secondary Font: Clean geometric sans-serif for body text (similar to Proxima Nova / Inter)
Primary Color: Black #000000
Secondary Color: White #FFFFFF
Accent Color: Electric Blue #2BBFFF (used in app, digital, and accent elements)
Additional Accents: Light Gray #F5F5F5 (backgrounds), Warm Stone #E8E0D8 (earth-tone collections)
Background Colors: White, off-white, deep black
CTA Color and Style: Black buttons with white text (primary), white buttons with black text (secondary). Clean rectangular, no rounded corners. Bold uppercase text inside.

PHOTOGRAPHY DIRECTION
Lighting: High-contrast studio lighting for product shots; natural/golden hour for lifestyle. Heavy use of dramatic side-lighting and rim-lighting to emphasize muscle definition and fabric texture.
Color Grading: Desaturated with selective warm tones. Moody blacks and clean whites. Occasional vibrant color pops matching collection palettes.
Composition: Tight crops on body/muscle groups. Full-body action shots mid-workout. Low angles for power. Symmetrical product flatlays.
Subject Matter: Fit, diverse athletes aged 18-30. Real gym environments, not sterile studios. Sweat, movement, intensity. Equal representation of men and women.
Props and Surfaces: Raw concrete, gym floors, metal racks, rubber mats. Minimal props — the body and the garment are the focus.
Mood: Intense, empowering, raw, aspirational but attainable.

PRODUCT DETAILS
Physical Description: Athletic apparel — leggings, shorts, sports bras, stringers, hoodies, joggers, t-shirts. Known for seamless knit construction, contouring lines, and second-skin fit.
Label-Logo Placement: Gymshark wordmark (custom sans-serif, all-caps) or shark head icon. Usually placed on center chest, upper back, or waistband. Small and clean, never oversized.
Distinctive Features: Seamless construction lines that contour the body. High-waisted leggings with V-shaped waistband. Camo prints. Vital Seamless collection. Signature conditioning cuts.
Packaging System: Black mailer bags, minimal black-and-white tissue paper, sticker seal with shark icon. Clean, minimal unboxing.

AD CREATIVE STYLE
Typical Formats: Short-form video (Reels/TikTok) dominates. Static ads use bold typography over lifestyle imagery. Split-screen transformations. Influencer/athlete features. Collection launch teasers with moody cinematography.
Text Overlay Style: Large bold condensed sans-serif in white or black. Minimal text — usually a headline and nothing else. Occasionally uses hand-drawn or brush-style accent text for energy.
Photo vs Illustration: Almost exclusively photography. No illustration.
UGC Usage: Heavy. Gymshark athletes and community members are the face of the brand. Reposts, challenges, transformation content.
Offer Presentation: Rare discounts. Major sales events (Birthday Sale, Black Friday) use bold countdown-style urgency. "Up to 70% off" in massive type on solid black backgrounds.

IMAGE GENERATION PROMPT MODIFIER
High-contrast fitness photography with dramatic rim-lighting on black or clean white backgrounds. Bold condensed sans-serif typography in black (#000000) or white (#FFFFFF) with electric blue (#2BBFFF) accents. Athletic subjects aged 18-30, diverse, muscular, mid-workout intensity. Raw gym textures — concrete, metal, rubber. Desaturated color grade with selective warm tones. Empowering, intense, community-driven energy. Gymshark wordmark in custom condensed all-caps sans-serif.
