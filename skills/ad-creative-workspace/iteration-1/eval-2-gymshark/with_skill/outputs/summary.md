# Gymshark Ad Creative — Summary

## Brand
- **Name:** Gymshark
- **URL:** gymshark.com
- **Output format:** Higsfield (plain text prompts)

## Brand DNA
Researched Gymshark's visual identity, voice, photography direction, and competitive positioning. Key brand attributes:
- **Colors:** Black (#000000), White (#FFFFFF), Electric Blue (#2BBFFF)
- **Typography:** Custom bold condensed sans-serif (all-caps headlines), clean geometric sans-serif body
- **Photography:** High-contrast, dramatic rim-lighting, diverse athletic subjects 18-30, raw gym environments
- **Voice:** Bold, empowering, community-driven, aspirational, youthful
- **Competitors:** Nike, Adidas, Lululemon, Under Armour — Gymshark differentiates via social-media-native community, DTC model, and affordable premium positioning

## Templates Generated
Three prompts created, each prepended with the 50-75 word Image Generation Prompt Modifier:

1. **Template 7 — Us vs Them** (4:5): Side-by-side comparison of generic gym wear vs Gymshark. Highlights fit, fabric tech, durability, and community.
2. **Template 21 — Bold Statement / Reaction Headline** (1:1): Black-to-electric-blue gradient with a raw motivational headline. Pure brand energy, no stats or reviews.
3. **Template 40 — Native / Ugly Post-It Note Style** (4:5): Casual unboxing scene with a hand-written post-it note on a Gymshark mailer bag. Maximum "found content" energy.
