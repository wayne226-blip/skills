# Article Templates

Structural templates for each article type. Use these as the skeleton — adapt headings and sections to fit the specific topic.

---

## Formatting Notes

These apply across all templates:

- **Currency:** Match the user's locale. Default to USD ($) unless told otherwise.
- **Billing cycles:** Always clarify monthly vs annual. Show both when available.
- **Links:** Use descriptive anchor text — `[Try Notion Free →](URL)` not `[Click here](URL)`.
- **Tables:** Keep to 4–6 columns max. Mobile readers can't parse wide tables.
- **Affiliate disclosure:** Add at the top if affiliate links are used.
- **Dates:** Always state when prices were last checked.
