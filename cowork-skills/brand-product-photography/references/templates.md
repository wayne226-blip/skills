# Product Photography Templates

20 product photography formats for brand-matched image generation. Each template has `[PLACEHOLDERS]` filled with brand-specific details from the Brand DNA document.

These templates produce Nano Banana / Gemini-ready prompts. They're designed for commercial product photography — e-commerce listings, social media hero shots, brand lookbooks, and catalogue imagery.

---

## Studio Product Shots

### 1. Hero Product Shot

The money shot. Clean, commercial, magazine-worthy. Every brand needs this one.

**Template:**
Use the attached images as brand reference. Match the exact product design, colours, and packaging precisely. Create: a premium studio product photograph on a [BACKGROUND — e.g. clean white gradient / soft warm cream / matte charcoal]. [YOUR PRODUCT — full description including shape, colour, label details, materials] positioned [ARRANGEMENT — e.g. at a slight 15-degree angle / standing upright / resting on its side] on a [SURFACE — e.g. polished marble slab / matte white acrylic / raw oak plank]. Shot on medium format digital, 85mm macro lens, f/5.6 for edge-to-edge sharpness. Soft diffused studio lighting from upper-left with subtle rim light from behind for edge definition. [BRAND COLOUR with hex] accent visible in [WHERE — e.g. the label / the cap / a thin colour strip at the base of the frame]. Subtle reflection on surface. No props, no distractions — the product IS the composition. Magazine-quality commercial photography. [MOOD — e.g. premium, clean, confident]. 1:1 aspect ratio.

**Ratio:** 1:1
